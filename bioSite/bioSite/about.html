A few quick answers to some burning questions everyone has

  • What is your favorite thing to do to relax?
    • ”In my free time I like to either play video games with my friends, or if everyone has time, we’ll meet up and play a boardgame together.”
  • What is your favorite boardgame?
    • ”That’s a tuff one. I usually like the co-op games where everyone works towards a goal, Betrayal at House on the Hill is a really fun version of that with some incredible twists so I would probably have to say that.”
  • What about your favorite video game?
    • ”This one’s even harder to answer (laughs). Best I can do on that is give you a top three since it will change daily. In no particular order there is Satisfactory, Final Fantasy XIV, and Chrono Trigger.”
  • You mentioned you tried to go to school already, where was that and for what?
    • ”My first try at school was through ITT Tech, which was later shut down for one reason or another. I wasn’t an ideal student at that time, so I didn’t get a lot out of the school anyway. I was going for the same thing I’m going for now, I think. I told them I wanted to make video games, so they put me in their “Video Game” course but I never got into the actual classes so I’m not sure if it was the same or more focused on Gaming in general rather than developing software.”
  • What would you consider to be the worst video game of all time?
    • ”Geez, I haven’t played them all but there are plenty of games out there that I don’t think are good that people LOVE. The most recent example of that is Expedition 33. It won game of the year and I don’t know anyone that doesn’t like it, except for me. I wouldn’t say it’s the worst game ever but it’s definitely not good, in my opinion.”
  • If you were unable to play either type of games, what do you think you would do in your free time?
    • It would have to be read. People keep trying to get me into audiobooks for Dungeon Crawler Carl. I tried to listen to the books, but my mind wanders after a few minutes of just listening to someone talk at me. I do plan on reading the books though but not for a bit.”
  • You mentioned board games; do you do any trading card games?
    • ”Actually, yes! We just got into, at least collecting, the Final Fantasy Magic: The Gathering cards. Both my Spouse and I are very much into the Final Fantasy series, so seeing all the artwork on the cards is a lot of fun for us. I used to play in middle school, and it was a lot of fun, but now it just seems too complicated for no reason for me to enjoy playing.”
  • So just Magic: The Gathering?
    ”For now. Although, we did just order some Yu-Gi-Oh! Cards to see if we can relive some of the fun we had playing in high school.”
  • Do you like PvP focused games? You specifically mentioned Co-op but nothing about working against other players.
    ”I’m a simple man, I see a bad guy, I try and kill the bad guy. I don’t do a lot of planning in any aspect of my life so strategy and backstabbing and all the aspects that the unknown of someone else can bring doesn’t appeal to me. I can see how people like it, but it’s just my jam. I’m also not competitive at all so having to “tie with everyone” to win doesn’t bother me.”
  • What do you plan to do once you graduate?
    • ”I’d love to get a job that pays well. If I can I’d like to stay with the company I’m working at now since I have 15 years with them, and I just saw that they have a Software Development position opened in the city I live in. Granted it was a level 6, but if they have 6’s here they more than likely have 1’s too.”
